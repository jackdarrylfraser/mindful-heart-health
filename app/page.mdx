## What is a mindful heart health diet?

A mindful heart health diet is an eating approach that focuses on nourishing your body with heart-friendly foods while staying present and intentional with each meal. 

It combines whole, minimally processed ingredients like vegetables, fruits, whole grains, lean proteins, healthy fats, and plant-based sources of fiber with a calm, attentive eating practice.

## Why it matters

This kind of diet supports heart health by lowering inflammation, improving blood pressure, stabilizing cholesterol, and helping maintain a healthy weight. 

It also encourages a balanced relationship with food, reducing stress around eating and making it easier to choose meals that benefit your heart over the long term.
